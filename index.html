HOW TO PLAY?

  • A word will be given, you need to find that particular word in the given square box.
  • Once after clicking that word, you need to find the second word, the second word starts with the ending letter of the first word.
  • In level 1, you need to find 5 words by using the above rule. After finding 5 words press any word to move to next level.
  • In level 2,you need to find 10 words. After finding 10 words press any word to move to next level.
  • In level 3,you need to find 15 words. After finding 15 words press any word to move to next level.
  • Like wise it goes.
  • There is only one pattern for the sequence. Find it.
  • If you couldn't find the sequence then your patteren is wrong.click "Restart" and the "Start" to try again.
